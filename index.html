About Me.

Hello, I'm Javiera, and I'm not your typical data practitioner. My roots are in STEM, and I take immense pride in being a data enthusiast and a bit of a nerd! I thrive on diving into complex problems and using data as my toolkit to find innovative solutions. What really drives me is the belief that the insights and solutions I uncover can bring about a positive transformation in the world. For this reason, I am naturally drawn to the fascinating industry of Educational Technology and Wellbeing.

In the field of EdTech, I see an opportunity to reshape education, making it more accessible, engaging, and effective. I believe in the potential of technology to bring happiness, health, and fulfillment into our lives.

Welcome to my journey, where I use data to make the world a better place.
